WHY I LOVE RADIOHEAD?

Radiohead wrote songs that I can identify with. I was a teenager the first time I heard their first two albums. The first two songs I love were entitled "Creep" and "Been Thinking About You."

Thomas Edward Yorke, or Thom Yorke is not just a singer that I admire, but a citizen of this planet that I want to emulate. He may come off as weird to many, but his akwardness is almost the same trait that I admire in the likes of the U.S. comedian Conan O' Brien.
